2. Equations 2X+3Y=9 & 7X+9Y=-6 have how many solutions?
Ans:- The system of equations 2X + 3Y = 9 and 7X + 9Y = -6 has a unique solution. This means that there is only one point (X, Y) that satisfies both equations simultaneously.
