Aptitude Assignment 2

1. What quantity of water should be added to the milk water mixture so that
the milk water ratio changes from 2:3 to 4:11. The quantity of milk in the
mixture is 40 litres?
Ans:- 60 liters of Water

2. Linear equation 2x+3y=0 meets the x & y-axis at the point?
Ans:- meets the x-axis at the point (0, 0) and the y-axis at the point (0, 0).

3. a & b are positive integers such that a^2-b^2=19. Find a & b?
Ans:- a=10 , b=9.

4. Find a^+b^3+c^3+3abc , where a+b+c=5 & , a^2+b^2+c^2=10?
Ans:- 125.

5. Sum of two, two-digit numbers is a perfect square. The digits of the first
two-digit number are two consecutive positive integers; also, when the digits
of the first number are reversed, the second number is formed. Find these
numbers & the square root of their sum.
Ans:-The two-digit numbers are 49 and 94, and the square root of their sum is 11.
